  • Only 5 nonstops exist MSY → LAX on Sunday. Mom's return dictates schedule flexibility, not yours.
  • Delta is the glue airline: one carrier covers outbound (Delta LGA 4:40 PM), Natalia's return (Delta JFK 7:55 PM), and Mom's return (Delta LAX 7:59 PM). Worth booking on delta.com for easier changes and SkyMiles.
  • Evening departures sync up Sunday: Natalia 7:55 PM + Mom 7:59 PM from MSY = one Uber to the airport, one emotional goodbye, done.
  • LGA is the best NYC airport for Brooklyn. ~30 min by Uber from Downing St. Avoids EWR (NJ taxes, farther) and JFK (farther east).

Hotels — Fri 4/17 → Sun 4/19 (2 nights, 1 room, 2 beds)

Reality check

Your $100/night target isn't realistic for this weekend. April 17-19 is the weekend right before Jazz Fest (4/24 – 5/3). Demand is at peak.

  • Booking.com reports 81% of NOLA properties unavailable for these dates
  • French Quarter specifically: 74% unavailable
  • Under $100/night with 2 beds = only suburban budget motels, 4-10 miles from downtown, rated 5-6/10
  • Airbnb is more expensive than hotels for NOLA ($140-250/night for similar quality) — NOLA short-term rental regulations constrain supply. Skip it.

Realistic budget: $150-200/night for a 2-bed room in the Warehouse/CBD area (10-15 min walk to French Quarter). French Quarter proper runs $380-500/night on this weekend.
